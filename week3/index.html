YOONCHAE

Yoonchae is a South-Korean singer under HYBE and Geffen Records. She is the maknae (youngest member) of KATSEYE. She participated as a contestant on the reality show The Debut: Dream Academy and ranked fourth place in the finale, thus becoming a member of the girl group KATSEYE.
